Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take to build a website?
    Most websites are completed in 13 weeks, depending on complexity and your feedback speed. Large or custom projects may take longer, but we always keep you updated.
  • Do you offer hosting and domain registration?
    Yes, we can recommend reliable local hosting, register your domain, or manage it for you as part of our maintenance plans.
  • Can I update my website myself?
    Absolutely! All sites come with easy instructions. For ongoing changes, our affordable maintenance plans are best.
  • What if I need something custom?
    We offer custom solutions for any business or nonprofit. Contact us for a tailored quote.
  • Do you provide support after launch?
    Yes! All packages include free support for a set period. You can extend support with our maintenance plans for peace of mind.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept EFT, credit/debit cards, and PayFast for South African clients. International payments are also supported.
